Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a donation?
You can make a donation by visiting our 'Donate' page and selecting your preferred payment method. We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, all donations to Donor Connect are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.
How is my donation used?
Your donation goes directly to supporting our various campaigns and initiatives. We ensure that at least 85% of all donations go directly to program expenses.
Can I volunteer instead of donating money?
Absolutely! We welcome volunteers for various projects. Please contact us for current volunteering opportunities.
How can I stay updated on your projects?
You can sign up for our newsletter on the homepage, follow us on social media, or check our blog for regular updates on our projects and impact.
